Track My Bus

Intro

Track My Bus lets you know how many stops away your bus is.

To track your bus you simply select your stop and then select the bus you want to track.

Searching for a Stop

Click on to find your stop.

Adding Buses to Track

Once you find your stop select the buses you want to track and save that stop.

Tracking your Bus

When your bus is 10 stops away it will appear in the app.

Select the bus to track it in realtime on the map.
